Network Rules and Acceptable Use Policy

Allowed use
This network may be used for normal web browsing, streaming, school work, communication, and personal projects.
Not allowed

  • Accessing, downloading, streaming, or distributing pornography or sexually explicit content.
  • Hacking, port scanning, vulnerability scanning, exploiting devices, or attempting to bypass security.
  • Attempting to access other devices on the network without permission, including printers, cameras, PCs, or servers.
  • Running disruptive services such as spamming, flooding, denial-of-service tools, or cryptocurrency mining.
  • Uploading, spreading, or using malware, spyware, or tools designed to damage or compromise systems.
  • Harassment, threats, intimidation, or targeting others using this network.
Privacy and monitoring
This network may log basic connection information for security and troubleshooting, including device identifiers, IP addresses, timestamps, and DNS requests.
Availability and enforcement
Access is provided as-is. Speeds may be limited, and access may be restricted or revoked at any time if these rules are violated.
